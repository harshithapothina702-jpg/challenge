🌍 How to Reduce Water Pollution in India

  • 💡 Educate communities about clean water practices.
  • 🚱 Avoid dumping chemicals or sewage into rivers/lakes.
  • 🌾 Promote organic farming to reduce harmful runoff.
  • 🏭 Enforce strict industrial waste treatment laws.
  • 🌊 Protect natural wetlands & watersheds.
  • 🚰 Harvest rainwater and reduce groundwater overuse.
