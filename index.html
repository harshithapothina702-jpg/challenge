How to Reduce Water Pollution in India

  • Properly treat industrial waste before releasing it into water bodies.
  • Promote awareness and community clean-up drives near rivers and lakes.
  • Reduce use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides in agriculture.
  • Implement strict regulations on sewage treatment plants.
  • Encourage water conservation and reduce plastic waste in water sources.
  • Support eco-friendly sanitation infrastructure in rural and urban areas.
