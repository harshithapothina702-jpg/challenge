How to Reduce Water Pollution in India

  • Enforce strict regulations on industrial effluents and sewage disposal.
  • Promote sustainable agriculture to minimize chemical runoff.
  • Increase public awareness through education campaigns and community programs.
  • Support water treatment plants and infrastructure improvements.
  • Reduce plastic usage and improve waste management systems.
  • Encourage rainwater harvesting and water conservation practices.
