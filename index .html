How to Reduce Water Pollution in India

  • Implement strict regulations on industrial waste disposal.
  • Promote awareness about proper sewage treatment and sanitation.
  • Encourage usage of eco-friendly farming techniques to reduce chemical runoff.
  • Organize community clean-up drives for rivers and lakes.
  • Invest in modern water treatment plants and infrastructure.
  • Ban or limit single-use plastics and harmful chemicals.
  • Promote rainwater harvesting and groundwater recharge.
